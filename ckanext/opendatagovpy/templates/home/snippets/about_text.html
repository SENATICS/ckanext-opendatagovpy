{{_('What\'s')}} datos.gov.py {{_('all about?')}}

{{ _('The Government of Paraguay is releasing public data to help people understand how their government works and how government resources management is done.') }} Datos.gov.py {{ _('is a portal that allows access to these open government data.') }} Datos.gov.py {{ _('gives any citizen, institution, enterprise or employee free, simple and dynamic access to open government datasets. The goal of the portal is to encourage transparency by helping citizens to understand and find out about the government\'s management of public goods. ')}}

{{_('By doing this, citizen participation is encouraged, seeking to accomplish a higher government efficiency, which attracts economic development. The content of the portal, made up of datasets, can also be used by application developers with the purpose of offering innovative and more accesible perspectives to data given by the government. Through this, citizens are empowered by being able of proposing improvements to government services based on new knowledge generation (Public value).')}}
